    • The Zone: Heretic Throne

    After two world wars, nations are divided and are forced to choose a side- west or east. The african scramble and asian intervention proved that the western forces of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization needed to prepare themselves evenmore incase of a direct soviet incursion into europe.

    Fierce competition between both sides lead to technological progression which created the western program to create a machine to manage the NATO's vast nuclear and defense arsenal. Years of development in absolute secrecy and the threat of soviet spies in american programs lead to increasing costs to protect and further their development. Unfortunately, the artificial intelligence program was soon cancelled and the bunker containing the project, abandoned.
